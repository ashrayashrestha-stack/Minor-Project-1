Frequently Asked Question

1. How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse through our categories, select the product you want, and click "Add to Cart." Once you've added all your desired items, go to the Cart page and click "Checkout." Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your order.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major debit/credit cards, digital wallets, and cash on delivery (COD) (where available). Secure online payments are processed through trusted gateways for your safety.

3. How long will it take for my order to arrive?

Delivery usually takes 3-7 business days, depending on your location and product availability. You'll receive tracking information once your order is shipped.

4. How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a tracking link via email or SMS. You can also track your order directly from the "My Orders" section of your account.

5. What is your return or exchange policy?

We offer a 7-day return or exchange policy for most products. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. To start a return, contact our support team with your order details.

6. Do you offer warranty on products?

Yes, products that come with manufacturer warranties will include the warranty information on their respective product pages. Please keep your invoice as proof of purchase.
